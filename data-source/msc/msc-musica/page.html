MSC MUSICA

Balconies to the world

Thoughtfully designed, every corner balances tranquil solitude with the pleasure of shared experiences, creating a ship that feels intimate, welcoming, and effortlessly sophisticated. Step aboard and embark on a journey reimagined, where every detail sparks curiosity and every experience is curated to nurture your wellbeing.
MSC Musica unveils its newest sanctuary: the MSC Yacht Club, opening in November 2026. An exclusive retreat that feels as private as it is exclusive. The journey to restoration continues at the completely reimagined MSC Aurea Spa and the upgraded MSC Gym Powered by Technogym®. Culinary exploration also takes centre stage with two new speciality restaurants - Butcher’s Cut and Kaito Sushi Bar - inviting you to embark on an exceptional gastronomic journey.

Main Features

Life on board

Life on MSC Musica is lovely from the moment you step on board. As soon as you see the central foyer’s three-tier waterfall and its see-through piano, suspended on a crystal floor above a pool of shimmering water, you know this is a special ship.

Life on board

Relax & Fitness

The journey to restoration starts at the completely reimagined MSC Aurea Spa, where ancient wellness traditions meet contemporary innovation to offer a serene escape for body and mind. From soothing thermal zones to restorative treatments, each element is designed to revive your energy and calm your senses. If you prefer to stay active, the upgraded MSC Gym Powered by Technogym® combines state-of-the-art equipment with panoramic ocean vistas, turning every workout into a breathtaking experience.

Relax & Fitness

Family & Kids

On MSC Musica, children and young adults enjoy the special treatment for which MSC Cruises is well known, with colourful indoor and outdoor play amenities, a pool of their own and separate Kids and Teens Clubs for a vibrant social life on board.

Family & Kids

MSC Yacht Club, a "ship within a ship"

MSC Musica unveils its newest sanctuary: the MSC Yacht Club, opening in November 2026. An exclusive retreat that feels as private as it is exclusive, it features 63 exquisitely designed suites, 24-hour butler and concierge service, a refined private Top Sail lounge crafted for quiet indulgence and your own private restaurant. Completing the experience is the private sundeck, a serene oasis reserved exclusively for MSC Yacht Club guests, featuring a grill and bar, perfect for leisurely afternoons in the sun, and a whirlpool where relaxation unfolds with uninterrupted sea views. Here, every moment is tailored to your desires and privacy and elegance converge seamlessly.

MSC WORLD ASIA

The Art of Cruising

Discover The Art of Cruising on board MSC World Asia, the new World Class ship from MSC Cruises.
MSC World Asia showcases MSC Cruises' unparalleled expertise in The Art of Cruising, blending refined craftsmanship with meticulous attention to detail.
This harmonious combination delivers the art of savouring, exploring and making memories.

itineraries

Set sail on a spectacular Mediterranean cruise on board MSC World Asia, discovering the best of Italy, Malta, Spain, and France, with convenient embarkation ports to start your journey.

Genoa

Discover the spectacular Aquarium of Genoa, savour local delights like pesto and focaccia, or escape to nearby Portofino for an unforgettable Riviera experience. Explore Genoa

Civitavecchia (Rome)

A living museum where ancient wonders like the Colosseum stand alongside Baroque masterpieces and the treasures of the Vatican Museums, making the city a timeless showcase of history and art. Explore Rome

Messina

Messina is your gateway to Sicily’s wonders, from the iconic Greek Theatre in Taormina to the breathtaking views of Mount Etna. Explore Messina

Naples

Naples bursts with energy, from its vibrant streets and world-famous cuisine to the ancient ruins of Pompeii and the sun-drenched beauty of Capri and Ischia. Explore Naples

Valletta

Valletta dazzles with incredible architecture, fortified streets, and Mediterranean charm, offering a gateway to Malta’s history, vibrant harbours, and stunning coastal views. Explore Valletta

Barcelona

Barcelona offers experiences and sights for all, from classical and modern art to daring architecture, from innovative design to ancient history, plus world-class gastronomy and an enviable coastline. Explore Barcelona

Marseille

Marseille pulses with cosmopolitan energy, from the lively cafés and the wooden boats of Le Vieux-Port to the sun-soaked landscapes and charm of surrounding Provence. Discover Marseille

MAIN FEATURES

The Art of Cruising

The new MSC Cruises World Class ship is designed for unique and unforgettable experiences at sea. Immerse yourself in a stylish, international atmosphere where you'll be seduced by the most charming and refined craftsmanship and attention to detail, on a ship designed for all guests and travellers.

The Art of Design

MSC World Asia blends traditional Asian architecture with contemporary design, celebrating the continent’s rich culture, art and landscapes. From the iconic 12-metre-long suspended Dragon sculpture and Koi Carp artworks in the World Promenade to art-filled corridors and cabins, every space inspires. Guests can also race down The Spiral @ Tree of Life, inspired by Singapore’s Gardens by the Bay.

The Art of Savouring

Enjoy a gastronomic voyage of discovery that offers everything from fine dining to casual and informal, with a choice of more than 40 bars, lounges and dining venues, including an incredible 6 speciality restaurants such as the Asian-inspired Kaito Teppanyaki Grill and Kaito Sushi Bar, the classic American-style steakhouse Butcher’s Cut, the Latin American street food-inspired Hola! Tacos & Cantina and new Pan-Asian Street Food fast-casual dining concept.

The Art of Entertainment

Revel in the excitement of award-winning activities for the whole family. Enjoy countless memorable experiences for children and families at the Kids’ Clubs, alongside entertainment for all ages, including two brand-new LEGO® rooms, a roller-skating arena in the all-new Clubhouse, the multi-purpose MSC Luna Park Arena, the Panorama Lounge and the World Theatre. Experience The Harbour, our stunning outdoor theme park, fly 50 metres above the sea on the Cliffhanger, and don’t miss the Adventure Trail ropes course.

The Art of Feeling well

Enjoy an array of relaxing treatments and facilities, creating the ultimate wellness escape at sea. Amenities include the MSC Cruises’ luxurious Aurea Spa with a comprehensive range of massages and beauty treatments, Gym powered by Technogym® with stunning ocean views, The Gentleman’s Barber, seven pools, including Zen pools at the aft of the ship, and 13 whirlpool baths.

ENTERTAINMENT

Guests of all ages will be able to enjoy awe-inspiring entertainment from morning to night. And as always, the MSC family offering features state-of-the-art facilities for kids with a dedicated area plus a range of award-winning kids' entertainment and tailor-made programmes designed for each age group.
Enriching the entertainment offering is the Family Aventura District, designed to give families even more ways to connect and enjoy shared moments.
The Clubhouse, an exciting space where families come together for high-energy fun, and the newly redesigned MSC Luna Park Arena complete the entertainment offer.

RESTAURANTS & BUFFET

MSC World Asia offers unexpected, interactive experiences. The art of elevating ingredients crafts dining experiences to meet global tastes for all guests and travellers including 6 speciality restaurants. In addition, with the new Pan-Asian Street Food fast-casual dining concept, every moment is thoughtfully crafted to immerse guests in the vibrant spirit of Southeast Asia. From authentic indoor Street Food seating, a rooftop Terrace with a relaxed, social atmosphere for casual bites, plus a complementary On The Go counter to enjoy your Pan-Asian delights on the go.
Guests also have the opportunity to discover 2 ample restaurant buffets on board, each offering a distinct ambience, open 24/7, and the 6 main dining restaurants.
Pan-Asian Street Food

The venue combines food, design, music and live cooking to create a multi-sensory environment, inviting guests on a vibrant culinary journey inspired by Southeast Asia.

Kaito Teppanyaki

Step in, choose your spot at one of four cooking stations and watch your delicious Japanese dishes come to life, right before your eyes.

HOLA! Tacos & Cantina

The new fast-casual street food dining concept, offers a selection of Latin American and Mexican-inspired dishes and drinks to be shared and enjoyed in a relaxed and friendly atmosphere, open for lunch and dinner.

Les Dunes Restaurant

Offering exquisite Mediterranean and international specialities and a change of menu everyday means that no two days are the same in our main restaurant.

Butcher's Cut

This American-style steak house serves select Linz heritage angus beef, gold standard in high-end angus beef thanks to the best genetics in breed. The highest quality product custom cut by master butchers and aged to perfection. The menu is accompanied by bold New World wines and cocktails with swagger.

Pizza & Burger

A wonderfully comfortable and casual place to eat. Watch our chefs at work while you enjoy the best of Mediterranean dining with a spectacular sea view.

MSC Yacht Club Restaurant

A private, luxurious restaurant exclusive to MSC Yacht Club Guests, where delectable fresh gourmet menus are created every day and served at your preferred time.

La Foglia Restaurant

In our main restaurant, we offer a unique culinary experience every day. With our exquisite Mediterranean and international specialties and a daily changing menu, we ensure that no two days are the same, providing you with a fresh and exciting dining experience each time.

Esagono / Hexagon Restaurant

A delightfully relaxed and cozy dining spot awaits you, offering the finest Mediterranean cuisine with a mesmerizing ocean view. Watch our skilled chefs showcase their culinary expertise while you savor your meal in a casual ambiance.

Bars & Lounges

A wide variety of more than 40 bars, lounges and cafes to suit any moods.
World-class concepts offered include an authentic Coffee Emporium with selections from around the world, the Masters of the Sea:
MSC Cruises’ classic British pub with a full-scale micro-brewery making MSC Signature beers on board.
The list goes on: guests can discover the elegant Fizz - Champagne Bar, a zesty juice bar with the freshest ingredients, a gelateria with a multitude of flavours to tempt sweet-tooths, the Southeast Asian-inspired cocktails and beverages where guests may enjoy scented jasmine mojitos, rose and strawberry daiquiris and matcha coladas alike.

Pan-Asian Street Bar

The bar features a selection of the most popular Southeast Asian-inspired signature cocktails.

All-Stars Sports Bar

This lively venue offers an authentic gameday atmosphere featuring larges screens, interactive games and an outdoor sea -facing terrace – perfect for sports fans and family gatherings.

Coffee Emporium

Explore the amazing contrasts between different blends of premium coffee, selected for their quality by coffee masters Lavazza.

Dolce Vita Bar

From traditional aperitifs, our Signature Cocktails to the contemporary classics, there is something to suit your taste.

Masters of The Sea

The Masters of the Sea pub has long been a favourite spot to grab a quick bite and to delight in the extensive beer list, but this will be the first time that it features an onboard brewery. 

Panorama Lounge

Grab your pre-show cocktail and watch this stylish two-deck venue transform into a spectacle of dazzling theater shows and interactive LED screens.

The Loft - Comedy & Live Music

If it's laughter you're after, the new comedy club The Loft - Comedy & Live Music brings you a line-up of the funniest comedians at sea, complemented by a programme of live music.

Fizz - Champagne Bar

Our carefully chosen and wide selection of fine champagnes, sparkling wines and champagne cocktails will tantalise your palate.

Zest Juice Bar

Zest up your life with fresh-pressed fruit and vegetable juices, smoothies, power shakes, fruit salads and quinoa fruit bowls.

MSC ORCHESTRA

Balconies to the world

Combining spacious, elegant interiors with fine dining and a varied entertainment programme, MSC Orchestra has long offered a voyage beyond expectations.
Now, that experience evolves into something even more exceptional. From March 2027, MSC Orchestra will unveil the MSC Yacht Club, a private world crafted for guests seeking the highest expression of comfort and distinction at sea. Completing the experience a newly reimagined MSC Aurea Spa a fully refurbished MSC Gym Powered by Technogym® and two new speciality restaurants promise unforgettable moments.

Itineraries

Book your MSC Orchestra cruise and enjoy the timeless beauty of the Mediterranean, sailing through crystal-clear waters and historic coastal cities.

MAIN FEATURES

Spa & Relaxation

Relaxation reaches new heights in the ship’s newly reimagined MSC Aurea Spa, where soothing treatments, thermal area, relaxing whirlpools and tranquil surroundings that restore both body and spirit. The experience continues in the Top 15 Exclusive Solarium, offering breathtaking panoramic ocean views and an elevated space to unwind in total serenity.

Spa & Relaxation

Sport & Fitness

For those who find their energy in movement, the fully refurbished MSC Gym Powered by Technogym® invites guests to stay active while taking in breathtaking ocean views, making every workout feel invigorating and inspiring. For those who love to stay active, the ship also features a basketball court, a tennis court, and a scenic jogging track.

Sport & Fitness

Family & Children

Kids can go wild to their heart’s content in the Jungle Adventure playroom, while teens get their own club too. Whatever your age and dreams, you can be sure they’ll come true for you, your friends and family on a cruise like no other with MSC Orchestra.

Family & Children

MSC Yacht Club, a "ship within a ship"

Discover the MSC Yacht Club, a private world crafted for guests seeking the highest expression of comfort and distinction at sea.
Within this exclusive retreat, 63 beautifully appointed suites offer absolute comfort, complemented by 24‑hour butler and concierge service, a dedicated private restaurant, and the elegant Top Sail Lounge, where quiet exclusivity and sweeping ocean views set the tone for your day. Enhancing the experience is the private sundeck, reserved solely for MSC Yacht Club guests, an elevated space featuring a grill and bar for relaxed moments and a whirlpool where the sea becomes your horizon of pure tranquility

Restaurants & Buffet

Gastronomy aboard MSC Orchestra is a journey in itself, from La Piazzetta and Four Seasons dining experiences to L'Ibiscus and Villa Borghese main restaurants, two new culinary destinations elevate this voyage. At Kaito Sushi Bar, masterfully crafted sushi and fresh, vibrant flavors celebrate the artistry of Asian cuisine, while the Butcher’s Cut invites a different kind of indulgence, where prime cuts are complemented by bold flavors. every meal a celebration of taste and craftsmanship.
